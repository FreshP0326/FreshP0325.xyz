# Black201

- Manager of **Momokan (桃罐)**

- Vice-owner of **Doujin Music Café**

---

### Who is Black201
**Born April 14, 2001.**

Discovered doujin music in elementary school, began self-learning guitar in middle school. Officially started music production career in 2019 with the goal of "creating unrestricted, diverse music."

In 2020, officially entered the doujin music scene with "Massage Seat" released in GROUND ATTACK!!! In 2022, founded the personal circle "Momokan (桃罐)" to pursue purer self-expression. In 2025, co-founded **Sequro** with AiSS and Nirotiy. To this day, he maintains a strong drive for creation, continuously exploring the vast world of music.

---

### About Momokan (桃罐)

**What is Momokan?**
Pink magical girls from various times and spaces gather here... In short, this is Black201's personal circle for releasing original works.

**Are all the aliases under Momokan you?**
Yes, all of them are me.

**Why do you have so many aliases?**
One reason is that it's fun, and of course I've been greatly influenced by HALLEY LABS and HITNET.

From a business perspective, having too many aliases often does more harm than good, because it's hard to invest enough energy in a single field and even harder for listeners to form a deep impression of you. However, the biggest benefit of the alias system is that it allows listeners to directly predict the music style by the "name"—you won't be surprised by UPTEMPO when expecting a ふわふわ (fluffy) track. Additionally, I want to give my music more narrative depth, which I'll talk more about when there's an opportunity.

- **Black201** — Mainly handles visual content creation, social media operations, and writing some not-so-great articles
- **DJ Momo** — Handles UK/Happy Hardcore and more pop-retro Rave music (like Bubblegum, Handzup, etc.)
- **The Operation** — Handles Kick-focused Hardcore / Hardstyle production
- **ちよもも (Chiyomomo)** — Handles Hard Trance and Freeform
- **oперація 27** — Inorganic music
- **ちよもも魔法使い (Chiyomomo Witch)** — Pure instrumental direction
- **FreshP** — Handles Vocal music creation
- **Darkness Peach** — Bass Music
